Anemia: Symptoms & Features

**Focus:** Restoring Iron and Vitamin B12 levels for sustained energy.

Core SymptomsChronic Fatigue, Paleness, Dizziness, Rapid Heartbeat.
Dietary Feature**Iron-rich veg** (Spinach, Lentils, Tofu, Chickpeas) + **Vitamin C** (Lemon, Orange) for maximum absorption.

Anemia: Cures & Management

Primary Cure**Prescribed Oral Iron Supplements** (e.g., Ferrous Fumarate) taken consistently.
B12 CureIf Pernicious Anemia, **B12 Injections** are necessary. Otherwise, fortified foods/oral tablets.
TipTake Iron supplements *at least* 1 hour away from dairy, coffee, or calcium tablets.

PMS: Symptoms & Features

**Focus:** Reducing inflammation, balancing mood, and managing fluid retention.

Core SymptomsIrritability, Anxiety, Bloating, Breast Tenderness, Headaches.
Nutritional FeatureHigh intake of **Magnesium** (dark chocolate, pumpkin seeds), **Calcium** (dairy, tofu), and **B6** (bananas, potatoes).

PMS: Cures & Management

Primary ReliefConsistent daily **Aerobic Exercise** (brisk walking, cycling) is a proven mood-booster.
Dietary ReliefReduce **Salt, Caffeine, and Alcohol** in the week before your cycle to minimize bloating and mood swings.
Medical OptionFor severe symptoms (PMDD), **SSRIs** or **Hormonal Birth Control** can offer significant relief. Consult a doctor.

Mental Wellness: Symptoms & Management

Your mind and body are connected. Prioritizing rest helps nutrient absorption.

Core SignsEmotional exhaustion, Loss of joy (Anhedonia), Difficulty relaxing.
Key Tool**CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy)** techniques to reframe negative thought patterns.

Mind-Body Cures & Tips

Stress Cure**3x3 Rule:** 3 deep, slow breaths, 3 times a day.
Anxiety ReliefUse the Pomodoro break time for a **"Grounding Exercise"** (Name 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste).
Fatigue LoopAcknowledge that **physical rest is mandatory** when Anemia flares. Lower your expectations for those days.

When You Get Bored, Even at 12 Night üåô

When you're restless late at night, choose an activity that matches your mood, not your screen time!

If you feel **STRESSED/ANXIOUS**:

**Book Recommendation:** *Bhagavad Gita* (or any short philosophical text). Focus on one passage for 10 minutes. (Calm)

**Activity:** **Gentle hand stretches** or **Mantra coloring** (repetitive, mindful action).

**Music:** Slow classical music (e.g., Debussy) or **Delta Waves** for sleep.

If you feel **LOW/SAD** (Mental Care needed):

**Book Recommendation:** An uplifting, gentle poetry collection or a short book on **gratitude journaling**.

**Activity:** Write down **5 things you accomplished today** (even tiny ones). Don't judge them, just list them.

**Music:** Acoustic covers of popular songs or **Lofi Hip-Hop** (soothing background noise).

If you feel **RESTLESS/ENERGIZED** (Need to wind down):

**Book Recommendation:** A light, engaging fiction book (like a cozy mystery) to distract the brain without intense focus.

**Activity:** **Plan the next day's schedule** (using the planner page). Lay out your clothes for the morning. (Productive, non-stressful prep).

**Music:** **Ambient nature sounds** (rain, ocean) or simple instrumental jazz.
